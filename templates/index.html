Water Pollution

Water has been widely known for being the solvent that is capable of dissolving various substances. Because of this, water can be easily polluted. When harmful substances are released into bodies of water, such as streams or rivers, they easily dissolve and mix with the water resulting in contamination. Contaminated Water can have severe health risks for both Wildlife and Humans.
